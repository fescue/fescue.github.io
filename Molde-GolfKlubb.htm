Molde GolfKlubb

Molde GolfKlubb in Romsdal county, Norway is a 9 hole golf course which opened in 2001.

Golf architect Caspar Grauballe has proposed changes to build 3 new holes including a dramatic quarry hole. Caspar Grauballe was EIGCA president from 2023 to 2025.
