Fall Line - East Course

Fall Line Golf Club East Course is designed by Oglivy Cocking Mead(OCM) in Georgia. The East Course is inspired by heathland golf courses in England.

Fairways are zoysia grass. Greens are planted in TifEagle bermudagrass.

Play from the first tee is shared with the West Course. The East course teeground plays down the fairway on the right to a par 4 of 425 yards. The tee ground also shares ground and plays as a practice putting green.
